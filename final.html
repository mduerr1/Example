2017-2018 season

A ranking of the ten best goalies from the 2017-2018 season. Based on statistics from NHL.com

Number 10: Antti Raanta (ARI)

Although Antti Raanta is a young and somewhat inexperienced goalie, he had an excelent season. Esspecially since he played for one of the worst teams in the league. In addition to making some spectacular saves, he also boasts 3 shutouts and a 2.24 GAA, tied for second in the league.
